## üì∏ EXTERIOR PHOTOS (3 Required)

### üè¢ Shot 1: Full Storefront (Primary)
**This is your main location photo - most important!**

**Timing:** 10am-2pm, sunny or bright overcast  
**Avoid:** Rain, snow, harsh shadows, sunset

**Steps:**
1. Stand across the street to get full building
2. Hold iPhone **VERTICALLY** (portrait orientation)
3. Use grid: Align building with vertical lines
4. Tap on storefront to focus (yellow box)
5. Slide brightness bar UP slightly (+1 or +2)
6. Take 5-10 shots from slightly different angles
7. Walk left/right to find the best perspective

**Composition:**
- ‚úÖ Center the main entrance/doors
- ‚úÖ Include sidewalk (shows accessibility)
- ‚úÖ Store name/signage clearly visible
- ‚úÖ Straight horizon line (not tilted)

**Avoid:**
- ‚ùå Your shadow in the shot
- ‚ùå Cars blocking the view
- ‚ùå People walking in front
- ‚ùå Trash cans, utility poles
- ‚ùå Harsh shadows from direct sun

---

### üö™ Shot 2: Entrance Close-Up
**Show welcoming, accessible entrance**

**Steps:**
1. Stand 10-15 feet from doors
2. Hold iPhone **HORIZONTALLY** (landscape)
3. Center the entrance doors

**Include:**

**Photo Specs:**
- Target size: 1920x1080px
- Format: JPEG

## ‚úèÔ∏è EDITING (5 minutes per photo)

### Use iPhone Photos App (Built-in)

1. **Open photo** ‚Üí Tap "Edit"
2. **Auto Enhance** (magic wand icon) - Start here
3. **Adjust Manually:**
   - **Light:** +10 to +20 (brighten)
   - **Contrast:** +5 to +10 (make it pop)
   - **Saturation:** +5 (slightly boost colors)
   - **Sharpness:** +10 (crisp details)
4. **Straighten:** Use level tool if tilted
5. **Crop:** Remove distractions at edges
6. **Done** ‚Üí Save

**Don't Over-Edit:**
- Keep it natural and realistic
- Slight enhancements only
- No extreme filters

## üö´ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Dirty Lens** ‚Üí Always wipe first
2. **Wrong Orientation** ‚Üí Match the guide (vertical for storefront)
3. **Not Tapping to Focus** ‚Üí Tap screen every shot
4. **Too Dark** ‚Üí Adjust brightness slider UP
5. **Tilted Horizon** ‚Üí Use grid lines
6. **Cluttered Background** ‚Üí Clean area first
7. **People in Frame** ‚Üí Wait for clear shot
8. **Your Shadow** ‚Üí Change position
9. **Harsh Shadows** ‚Üí Shoot 10am-2pm, not early/late
10. **Rushing** ‚Üí Take 5-10 shots per angle

---

## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Golden Hour is a Myth for Storefronts:** Midday (10am-2pm) is better for even lighting
2. **Bracket Your Shots:** Take same photo at -1, 0, +1 brightness
3. **Walk Around:** Don't shoot from one spot, move around
4. **Use Both Hands:** More stable than one-handed
5. **Lean Against Something:** Wall, car, pole for stability
6. **Review on Computer:** Photos look different on big screen
7. **Shoot More Than You Need:** 3-5x the final count
8. **Return if Conditions Suck:** Don't force it on a cloudy/rainy day

## üìû QUESTIONS?

**Fix:** Move further back to get full signage in frame

**Issue:** Too many reflections on windows  
**Fix:** Change angle slightly, avoid direct sun on glass
