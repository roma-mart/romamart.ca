# Store Photography Guide

> Guidelines for capturing Roma Mart store images

## Overview

High-quality store photography helps showcase Roma Mart locations and creates a welcoming impression for customers.

## Equipment

### Recommended

- DSLR or mirrorless camera
- Wide-angle lens (24-35mm)
- Tripod for stability
- External flash (optional)

### Acceptable

- Modern smartphone (iPhone 12+ or equivalent)
- Good natural lighting

## Shooting Guidelines

### Exterior Shots

1. **Time of Day:** Golden hour (morning/evening) preferred
2. **Weather:** Clear or partly cloudy
3. **Angle:** Straight-on or slight angle showing signage
4. **Include:** Store signage, entrance, parking area

### Interior Shots

1. **Lighting:** Use natural light when possible
2. **Clean:** Ensure store is tidy and organized
3. **Products:** Well-stocked shelves
4. **People:** Get consent for any recognizable individuals

### Key Shots Needed

| Shot | Purpose | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Storefront | Location page | Include signage |
| Entrance | Welcome | Show accessibility |
| Aisles | Products | Well-stocked |
| Counter | Service | Clean, organized |
| RoCafé | Coffee bar | If applicable |

## Technical Settings

### Camera

- Aperture: f/8-f/11 for depth
- ISO: As low as possible (100-400)
- Shutter: 1/60 or faster (handheld)

### Post-Processing

- White balance correction
- Exposure adjustment
- Straighten if needed
- No heavy filters

## File Specifications

| Deliverable | Format | Size |
|-------------|--------|------|
| Original | RAW/JPG | Full resolution |
| Web | WebP | Max 1920px wide |
| Thumbnail | WebP | 400x300px |

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do

- ✅ Capture store at its best
- ✅ Show accessibility features
- ✅ Include brand signage
- ✅ Get multiple angles

### Don't

- ❌ Include identifiable customers without consent
- ❌ Show messy or empty shelves
- ❌ Over-process images
- ❌ Use extreme angles
